ABOUT ASPAHLT GREEN

Located in Upper East Side

Coming to the United States was a struggle for me at first but I knew the one of the things I wanted to keep stable in my life was swimming. Asphalt Green was my choosen landmark becasue it holds a sense of familiarity, whether its the chlorine or the 50 meter pool. It serves as a second home. I spend almost 11 hours a week there and it has a cafe or my forever hungry stomach!!

FACTS ABOUT ASPHALT GREEN

1. This facility used to be the Municipal Asphalt Plant, hence the name Asphalt. It was then converted into a gymnasium and multi-purpose classrooms. The Aqua center was added last.

2. The Stanley Issac's Waterproofing Program is held at the Aqua center and volunteers, one being myself, work together to provide free swim lessons to the Stanley Issacs community.

3. The deepest side of the pool in the Asphalt Green Aqautic Centre is 16 feet deep!! I do not recommend going to the bottom, your ears will hate you.
